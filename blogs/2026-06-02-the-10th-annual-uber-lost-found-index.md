Uber celebrates a decade of its Lost & Found Index, revealing that phones remain the most commonly forgotten item, with New York City ranking as the forgetfulness capital. The company has introduced an improved feature allowing riders to request return trips directly through the app to recover lost belongings in select U.S. markets, with nationwide expansion planned by year's end.
